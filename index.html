Learning HTML5!

March 18th, 2017 by Ed Luong

Today I finished the tutorial that Duncan Aitken had created on a website called html-5-tutorial that is dedicated to teaching HTML5.

I found the material very easy to digest such as the language was conversational while being highly informative.

Aitken does a great job explaining that a website has two types of audience that will visit your website. Such as normal human visitors as well as spiders or bots (for example: Googlebot). He also goes into detail on how to layout your website for SEO purposes. Check out his website if you're interested!
